I’m Eleonora Sorokina - the UX/UI Designer based in Voronezh, Russia. My background is 5 years in Architecture, in which I hold a Bachelor of Architecture with distinction

During my studies at the university, I was a very proactive person. And I tried to get as many diverse experiences as possible to test my skills and learn something new about myself. As a result, I found out that I am a team player and that my interest is focused on the digital space.

As an UX/UI Designer, in development, I usually start with researching and building hypotheses. After defining the main tasks, I am engaged in prototyping. I believe, understanding human needs is fundamental in creating meaningful and emotional products.

Now I am actively looking for a job in Krakow or Warsaw as an UX/UI Designer. Feel free to say hi!
