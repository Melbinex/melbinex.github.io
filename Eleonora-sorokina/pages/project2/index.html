territory of health

arteza / 2021 Moscow / workshop

design rules for healthcare territories were formulated
the project was demonstrated on the festival «Open City»

THE TASK AND PURPOSE OF THE WORKSHOP

Investigate and identify key issues improvement of territories of social objects

To formulate the principles of designing the territory of social objects taking into account modern requirements and a view of the future development of social objects

To impose the invented principles on the proposed territory in the form of conditional design solutions

TEAM 3: HEALTH CENTER

Area of the territory:
1.2 hectares;

Buildings on the site:
connected 2 medical buildings, courtyards;

Special condition: direction - rehabilitation, restoration; Part of the territory is an object of cultural heritage;

Category of users: hospital patients, medical personnel, technical workers, urban residents, low-mobility groups of the population.

Team members: Natalia Kalinina (RUDN), Eleonora Sorokina (VSTU), Elena Skakunova (MArchI)

analytics

our team analyzed the territories of healthcare facilities and identified the main problems from them
